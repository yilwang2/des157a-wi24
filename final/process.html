Usability Test

Tests:

1. Focus on the images first, and write down your first impression of what the pictures are about when you see them.

2. Find information about "Roses", "Orchid" and "The Starry Night".

3. Find my contact information.

Interview Questions:

1. What do you think my website is about if you just look at the pictures?

2. Please describe the process by which you found the Lego's information.

3. Which close button did you use more often? The "x" button or the "close" button?

4. Is my contact information easy for you to find?

5. What type of pictures of these Legos would you like to see to know more about them?
