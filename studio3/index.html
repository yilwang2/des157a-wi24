The Game of PIG

Enjoy the game with Penguin and Polar Bear!

There are two players Penguin and Polar Bear. The player whose turn it is rolls the dice. The total of the roll is added to the current player's score, unless either die comes up as a "one". If this happens, this player's turn is over, and it is the other player's turn. After each roll, the current player can either roll again, (assuming a "one" was not rolled) or can pass to the other player. The first player to get 30 points or higher wins. If you roll two "ones" (snake eyes), your current score gets zeroed out.
